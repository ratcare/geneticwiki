gene: a segment of DNA that codes for a specific trait. allele: an allele is a form of a gene, typically represented by letters, ie. "A" or "a". each parent gets one allele. genotype: the full representation of one's genes and alleles. for example, a full genotype might look like "AABbCCddEe". the genotype for a single trait may only look like "aa" or "Cc" phenotype: the physical expression of a gene or allele. these can be seen by the naked eye. a rat's phenotype might be a dumbo hooded agouti, or a rex russian blue.
